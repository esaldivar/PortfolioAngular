04.JavaUnscripted Podcast

While on the journey to start a new career in tech, Sam Arnold, Charlie Malave, and Eric Saldivar started a podcast called JavaUnscripted.

The podcast that talks about the journey we take to learn to code, to become proficient, and the challenges we face. Not strictly a podcast about tech but also about life experiences and the experiences of our guests.

Some of the type of guests we have had include:

  • a Senior software engineering manager
  • a Technical recruiter
  • a Coding Bootcamp Instructor
  • a Military veteran
  • a Career transitioner
  • a Coding bootcamp graduate
